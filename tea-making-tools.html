Tea-Making Tools

There are so many tools and gadgets available for making tea. Below are a few of my recommendations for making the perfect cuppa.

  • Cuisinart Perfect Temp Cordless Programmable Kettle
    This kettle is a must-have. It has six pre-programmed heat settings for different types of tea (and even french press coffee!) so you never have to boil water and wait for it to cool down or monitor the temperature.
  • Tuffy Steeper
    I use this steeper whenever I may a single cup of tea. As someone with a small kitchen, I am always looking for space-saving solutions, and this steeper folds down to 1".
  • Tea Wallet
    If you travel a lot, or just always like to have your favorite tea on you, the tea wallet is for you! I am not a fan of airplane tea and make sure to pack my tea wallet whenever I fly.
  • Tea Cozy
    The tea cozy is essential if you make pots of tea. I recommend Thistledown Tea cozies for their durability and the length of time they keep your tea hot.
